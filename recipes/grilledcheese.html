Grilled Cheese (And Tomato Soup If You're Lucky)

About

The grilled cheese sandwich has to be one of the most iconic, simple, and delicious meals of all time. Throw in a tomato soup on the side, and you've got yourself a meal to die for.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Two slices of cheese
  • Butter
  • Optional: Can of tomato soup and some milk to add to it

Steps

  1. If cooking tomato soup, begin by adding it to a pot. Then fill the can with milk or water, and add that to the pot. Put it on medium heat, and stir until hot.
  2. Add butter to the pan and put on medium to high heat.
  3. Apply cheese slices to the bread, forming a sandwich.
  4. Add grilled cheese to pan. Flip occasionally until browned, with cheese melting. Adjust head as needed.
  5. Enjoy!
