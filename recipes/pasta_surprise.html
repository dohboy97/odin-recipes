Pasta Surprise

About

Another clasic meal - pasta noodles of any variation, whatever veggies you want, and tomato sauce. Is even better when used in alternation with the tofu surprise - so you never get sick of either!

Ingredients:

  • Pasta noodles
  • Tomato or pasta sauce
  • Assorted vegetables
  • Butter or cooking oil
  • Salt for taste
  • Optional: A source of protein, such as chicken or tofu

Steps

  1. Begin by boiling pasta in a pot.
  2. While it's boiling, prepare your other ingredients for frying.
  3. If using chicken, bake it at 375 for 30 minutes.
  4. If using tofu, chop it along with your vegetables, and begin frying it with oil or butter for about ten minutes prior to adding the vegetables, or until browned.
  5. Add vegetables, and cook until done.
  6. Add vegetables, protein if used, and pasta together. Add salt for flavor as needed, and mix in pasta sauce for an unextraordinary, yet reliably solid meal
  7. Enjoy!
